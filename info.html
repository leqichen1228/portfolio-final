    Hi, my name is Lena. I’m a master’s student at Parsons School of Design in NYC. Majoring in communication design, I focus on UI/UX study with an emphasis on product strategy thinking. I’m motivated by creating products that truly elevate user experience.

    Growing up in Shenzhen, China, and having been living in New York for 5 years now, I’m always surrounded by various kinds of culture, environment and people. I like to observe people’s behaviors and the way they approach things, talk to each other, or use technology. Curiosity enables me to think critically of the ordinary phenomena happening around.

    I’m a good listener. For me, listening to user stories is crucial for discovering and solving real-life problems. By documenting how a user changes in different stages of my project, I’m able to tell if my product truly improves their life.

    My UI/UX practices originated from my interest in brand strategy design. Before 2021 I worked as a freelance graphic designer on branding and packaging projects. I enjoy building and enhancing brand identity with research and user studies.
